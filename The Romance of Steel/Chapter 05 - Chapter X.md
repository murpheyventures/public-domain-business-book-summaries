## Summary

Its industrial army of twenty thousand, mostly negroes, made the United States richer by seventy-five millions in 1905, piling up twelve million tons of coal, sixteen hundred thousand tons of iron, and more than a hundred thousand tons of steel rails. These men have built up the four great companies that control the situation — the Tennessee Coal, Iron and Railroad Company, the Republic Iron and Steel, the Sloss-Sheffield, and the Alabama Consolidated.
