## Summary

Thus, the unit of waiting is said to be a year- pound, and the unit of labour a year-labourer.^ It would seem, therefore, that, if uncertainty -bearing, as a factor of production, is to stand on a level with waiting and labour, it must somehow bear a relation to time analogous to that which they bear. The great variety of schemes of uncertainty, which uncertainty-bearing in general is thus found to include, might seem at first sight to vitiate the attempt, which was made in an earlier section, to treat " the factor uncertainty-bearing " and " the factor waiting "*on the same footing. In order to render this procedure legitimate, all that we need do is to select in an arbitrary manner some particular sort of labour as our fundamental unit, and to express quantities of other sorts of labour in terms of this unit on the basis of their comparative values in the market. The exposure of a pound to a particular arbitrarily chosen scheme of uncertainty can be selected as a fundamental unit, and the exposure of resources to any scheme of uncertainty can be reduced, on the basis of comparative market values, to its equivalent in terms of this unit.
