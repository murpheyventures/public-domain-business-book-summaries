## Summary

This objection to public intervention in industry applies both to intervention through control of private companies, and to intervention through direct public operation. The force of this argument for non-interference by public authorities is, clearly, not the same at all times and places ; for any given kind of public authority will vary, alike in efficiency and in sense of public duty, with the general tone of the time.
