## Summary

In this important particular, animate force differs from inanimate force--the power of man, coming from within and expressing itself outwardly, is of another sort from the force of Shimose powder, which awaits some influence from without to explode it. Not only must we discern the difference between human force and mere physical force, but we must not confuse its real essence with some of the things that may--and may not--accompany it. Let not your force "die a-borning,"--bring it to full life in its conviction, emotional tension, resolve, and propulsive power. _Can Force be Acquired?_ Yes, if the acquirer has any such capacities as we have just outlined.
