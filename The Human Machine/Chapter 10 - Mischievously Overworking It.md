## Summary

For example, in the ordinary household the amount of machine horse-power expended in fighting for the truth is really quite absurd. I have formed myself into a select society for the propagating of the truth about the rent of the Joneses' new flat, and my wife has done the same. Ultimately my wife (or my husband) will learn the essential cosmic truth about the rent of the Joneses' new flat. Besides, nobody has appointed me sole custodian of the great truth concerning the rent of the Joneses' new flat.
