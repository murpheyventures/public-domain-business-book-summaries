## Summary

Stripped of all the details, the task was to find out whether or not the manufacturer might profitably engage in the making of hard rubber tires for industrial trucks and trailers. And when the analysis was done it told the manufacturer everything from where the rubber grew to where the money went to and came from. Well, well, the rubber manufacturer went out in the byways and got him a young man who was told to find out, if he could, whether or not there was any market for hard rubber tires on gas and electric industrial trucks, tractors and trailers, and allied equipment. Outside of the truck and tractor people, he found the equipment makers opposed to hard rubber tires.
