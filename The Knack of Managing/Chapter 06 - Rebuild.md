## Summary

The immediate end, this time, is to rearrange the pieces so that the job to be done can be done in the most effective way--the way that saves the most effort, the most time, the most money--the way which, in your business--and in _yours_ and YOURS--leads to NET PROFITS. In short, we must be sure we are taking things to pieces, not for the sake of taking them to pieces, but purely and simply _to find out what has to be done_. And, in like manner, a PLAN without an ANALYSIS isn't worth the paper it's typed on. But Psmith without Pbrown's analytical ability, or Pbrown without Psmith's capacity for planning how to get things done, isn't worth his weight in gold to _any_ business enterprise.
