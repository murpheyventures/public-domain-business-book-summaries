## Summary

A business man’s rating in the business community likewise rests on the pecuniary magnitude of his holdings and his transactions, not on the mechanical serviceability of his establishment or his output; and this business rating is a large part of the business man’s every-: day ambition. Business enterprise, it is true, proceeds on meta- physical grounds and is swayed by considerations of nominal wealth rather than by considerations of material serviceability ; but, none the less, business enterprise and business metaphysics control the course of industry.
