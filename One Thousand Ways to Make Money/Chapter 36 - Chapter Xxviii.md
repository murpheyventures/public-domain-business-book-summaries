## Summary

Every year by neglect, poor economy and extravagance, material is lost which if saved would be enough to make many people rich. In Germany, what is known as the Tonner system saves this richest of fertilizers; and the time is ripe for some one in this country to save this enormous waste and make himself many times a millionaire in the Book of Wealth. WASTE OF COAL ASHES.--Two hundred million tons of coal are consumed annually in this country. WASTE OF GARBAGE.--Shoreditch, population, 124,000, a borough of London, by a new system for the disposal of garbage, called the Dust Destructor, saved in one year $11,000, or enough to defray the expense of its electric lights.
