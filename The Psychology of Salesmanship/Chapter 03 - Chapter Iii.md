## Summary

A person filled with enthusiasm seems to move and act from the very centre of his being--that part which we mean when we say "soul." There is a wonderful power in rightly directed enthusiasm, which serves not only to arouse within one his full powers, but also tends to impress others in the direction of mental contagion.
