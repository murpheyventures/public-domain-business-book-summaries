## Summary

MEXICO Prior to the invasion of Mexico by the Spaniards, the Aztecs who inhabited the country, had developed a wonderful system of religion, education, civilization and government. The Spanish possessions in Mexico and Central America were united for the purposes of government, and a viceroy appointed first in 1535, this method for the control of these colonies being used until 1821. Spain sent an army to regain Mexico in 1829 but was utterly defeated, within three months after landing, and ultimately the Spanish Crown recognized the independence of Mexico, Dec. 28, 1836. [Illustration: MEXICO] April 21, 1836, Texas seceded from Mexico and was annexed to the United States in 1845, following which Mexico went to war with its northern neighbor, was conquered and had her capital occupied by American troops.
