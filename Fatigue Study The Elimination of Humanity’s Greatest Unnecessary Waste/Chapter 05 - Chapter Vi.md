## Summary

A fatigue museum is a collection of devices for and information concerning the elimination of fatigue, or for affording rest for overcoming fatigue. This de- vice helped make it possible to divide each hour into work periods and rest periods ; and at the same time into standing and sitting periods, — thus not only eliminating unnecessary fatigue, but providing an efficient means for recovery from necessary fatigue.
