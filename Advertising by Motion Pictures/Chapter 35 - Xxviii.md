## Summary

BOOSTING YOUR BUSINESS WITH AN ADVERTISING MOTION PICTURE Are you a storekeeper who would blazon the path of originality, yet would not object to the increased outlay? The advertising powers of the motion picture have been fully realized by the large manufacturers of this country, but the average retailer has confined himself to the slide. With a film, however, you can cause a small-sized sensation in your town, for folks will look upon your effort as a home product. Now, if you approach local motion-picture exhibitors in the right spirit, they may not demand a fee for showing your advertising motion picture, as they realize too well the value of anything with a local appeal.
