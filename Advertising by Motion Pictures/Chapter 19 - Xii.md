## Summary

EQUIPPING A PRIVATE MOTION-PICTURE THEATER FOR BUSINESS PURPOSES The manufacturer who intends adopting the motion picture as his advertising offspring for all time will find it necessary to install a private theater in his office building or manufacturing plant, according to what he may decide suits him best. The authorities throughout the country are getting exceedingly strict anent the showing and storing of films, so the room selected for the purpose should be made as fireproof as possible, for films are mighty inflammable. But, after you have equipped your private theater, it is best to call in your fire insurance agent to investigate and discover what his company’s action will be in the matter.
