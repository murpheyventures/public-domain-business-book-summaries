## Summary

Which would you prefer to boost a brand of shoes by--an industrial film, a comedy photoplay or a trick film? Keith Company, manufacturers of Walk-Over shoes, introduced themselves to the motion-picture public with “The Making of a Shoe,” said film being exactly one-reel in length. Their film, “A Footwear Romance,” featured, to use a studio term, Ruth Stonehouse and Bryant Ashburn.
