## Summary

Catch phrases are also good plot germs, and are capable of being worked in the same manner as trademarks. Not a few of our leading manufacturers have familiar persons in connection with their standard articles. Siemen Brothers, an English firm, brought their well-known “Wotan” maid and “Tantalum” man to life in a film. It was a comedy, and the plot’s mission, besides introducing these figures, was to bring home how 75 per cent. of the electric-light bill could be saved by the “Wotan” and “Tantalum” lamps.
