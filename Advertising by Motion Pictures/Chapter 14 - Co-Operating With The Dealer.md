## Summary

In the case of a proprietary line, the dealer has to be “roped in” before a successful appeal can be made to the public. He would be first advised of the forthcoming motion-picture campaign through the medium of his favorite trade journal. And, unless it is localized, he will probably regard it as of no consequence to him. When the Jewell stoves and ranges were, a short time ago, boosted by motion pictures in numerous towns, the two-reel film, occupying about thirty minutes on the screen, which depicted the various processes in the making of the goods, was exhibited after the ordinary program was over, a small fee being paid for the privilege.
