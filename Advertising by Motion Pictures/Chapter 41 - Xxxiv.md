## Summary

OBTAINING THE BEST RESULTS FROM SLIDE ADVERTISING Not even the most successful business man makes good in every enterprise he undertakes. The mistake too often made is to treat the motion-picture show as a new thing--a kind of scientific toy--but as a popular form of amusement it has long discarded its long pants. The photoplay, as a matter of fact, has settled down to enjoy a long and flourishing career, with strict adherence to business principles. Audiences appreciate these slides when they do not number too many, as they are the means of affording them something to turn their attention to instead of having nothing to do but idly gaze around the hall.
