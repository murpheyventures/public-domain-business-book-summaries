## Summary

A conservative estimate places the number of fans in this country at twenty millions, which number support the twenty thousand photoplay theaters. He gave over his window display to photographs of famous film players and arranged them in an artistic manner. It might hardly be a sound business policy to display only photographs in your store window to the total exclusion of your ordinary wares, for the underlying idea to get passers-by to view the goods set out in the window and act as a sort of temptation to buy. I am not acquainted with any instances where retailers have adopted the preceding plan, but if same can bring full houses on dull nights to motion-picture theaters that distribute such pictures to spectators, then it is a positive thing that it will help along your slack days.
