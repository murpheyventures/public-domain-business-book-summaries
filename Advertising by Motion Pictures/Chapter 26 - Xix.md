## Summary

CLINCHING AGRICULTURAL MACHINERY SALES BY MOTION PICTURES The manufacturer of heavy agricultural machinery and merchandise of an intricate nature is placed considerably at a disadvantage, yet, by enlisting the aid of the versatile motion picture, he can greatly improve his selling tactics. The exhibition of the picture to a number of the farmers at the same time saved much of the salesman’s time and trouble, and clinched a whole lot more business in the bargain. Tests were given in stump blasting, deep plowing, tree planting, ditching, etc., and effectively shown to farmers at institutes, land shows, State and county fairs, and on other suitable occasions. The demonstrations are cramped and can never be so thorough in scope as were a special cinema show to be erected and demonstrations given at stated intervals by means of a film.
