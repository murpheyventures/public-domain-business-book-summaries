## Summary

FUTURE DEVELOPMENTS OF ADVERTISING BY MOTION PICTURES There is a great future before advertising by motion pictures, and it is only with the most careful deliberation that I have concluded in what way the future developments will assume shape. The path of the pioneer may be speculative, but it need never be, if the alert advertiser studies the other end of the business, where photoplays are made and exhibited for entertainment purposes. One thing to be said in favor of the film is that it is a perfect paradise for the honest advertiser, for, as yet, fraudulent advertising has not been allowed to mingle with the genuine. Should this type of business man be unscrupulous enough, he might have his conditions of his plant pictured as ideal, by faking the whole thing in a film studio.
