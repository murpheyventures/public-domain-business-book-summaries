## Summary

Behind many commercial undertakings there is romance. It may be forgotten in the press of business, but human interest is too valuable as a publicity stunt to be overlooked. It is quite possible that these romantic stories are not appreciated at their full value until a motion-picture publicity expert comes along and squeezes all the “juice” out of them. The motion-picture industry has made its marvelous progress through the lifelike stories that predominate in the picture theaters.
