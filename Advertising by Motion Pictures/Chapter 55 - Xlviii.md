## Summary

NAMING SODA-FOUNTAIN CONCOCTIONS AFTER MOVIES Druggists should not be backward in availing themselves of one of the strongest forces of modern times--the motion picture. It is a poor town that does not contain a photoplay theater, and in most towns they are as plentiful as druggists--one on every few blocks. The proportion of motion-picture goers in this country is one to every five inhabitants, and, however it may work out in your town, it is practically certain that the majority of men, women and children spend their evenings at the motion-picture show. Why not introduce a Lillian Walker sundae, an Edward Earle frappe and a Charlie Chaplin soda?
