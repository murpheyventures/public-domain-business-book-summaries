## Summary

Now, it is doubtless this continued success of the Soviet administration in Russia that has thrown this ecstatic scare into the Guardians of the Vested Interests in America and in the civil- ized countries of Europe. There is nothing to be gained by denying that the Russian Soviet has achieved a measure of success; indeed, an aston- ishing measure of success, considering the ex- tremely adverse circumstances under which the Soviet has been at work.
