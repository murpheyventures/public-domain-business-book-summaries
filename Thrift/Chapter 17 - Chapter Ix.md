## Summary

"The sober comfort, all the peace which springs From the large aggregate of little things; On these small cares of daughter, wife, or friend, The almost sacred joys of Home depend."--_Hannah More_. "Know when to spend and when to spare, And when to buy, and thou shalt ne'er be bare." "He that despiseth little things, shall perish by little and little."--_Ecclesiasticus_.
