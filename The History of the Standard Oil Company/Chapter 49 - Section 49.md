## Summary

Second. — The total amount of the capital of the said association is $625,000, and said capital shall be paid at the times and in the manner following, to wit : Twenty- five per cent, thereof on the second day of December, A.D. The whole of said capital shall be paid in lawful money to the treasurer of said association at the principal office or place of business of said association at Titusville, Pennsylvania. The location of the business to be conducted [296] APPENDIX, NUMBER XXXVII by said association is at the city of Titusville, in the county of Crawford, and state of Pennsylvania, where the principal office or place of business of said association is established and shall be maintained. Fourth. — Each party hereto shall make to the other daily reports showing all crude petroleum received at the refineries named in said schedule, and when, where and from whom received, and all crude petroleum exported therefrom, and when, where and to whom delivered.
