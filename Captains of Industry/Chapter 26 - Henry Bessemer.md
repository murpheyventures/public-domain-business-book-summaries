## Summary

Iron is iron; but steel is iron mixed with carbon. As crystallized carbon or diamond is the hardest of all known substances, so also the blending of carbon with iron hardens it into steel. Our machinery was better, and pure charcoal was placed in the crucible instead of wood; but the process was long and costly, and only small pieces of steel were produced at a time. The next question was as to the compensation of the young inventor, and he was given his choice either to accept a sum of money or an office for life in the stamp office of four thousand dollars a year.
