## Summary

Direct affirmation as to the value of the goods offered may, in general, be the most effective form of expression, but the direct command could be used with profit be- cause of its superior value in attracting atten- tion and in begetting immediate action. One day I read an article by Professor Scott in Mahin's Maga- zine, in which he showed that if the words ' Cut this coupon out and mail it to-day ' were used instead of 4 Use this coupon,' there would be a larger number of replies. 80 VALUE OF THE RETURN COUPON One psychological value of the return coupon is that it attracts attention.
