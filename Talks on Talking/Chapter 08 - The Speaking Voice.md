## Summary

A musical quality of voice depends chiefly upon directing the tone towards the hard palate, or the bony arch above the upper teeth. Under such circumstances it is better to rest the voice, since in any contest of the kind the voice will almost surely be vanquished. For all-round vocal development this practice should be done with varied force and inflection, and on high as well as low keys of the voice. The best remedy for a weak voice is to practice daily upon explosives, expelling the principal vowel sounds, on various keys, using the abdominal muscles throughout.
