## Summary

To every thing there is a season, a time to keep silence, and a time to speak. Set a watch, O Lord, before my mouth; keep the door of my lips. Be swift to hear, slow to speak, slow to wrath. Let your speech be always with grace, seasoned with salt, that ye may know how ye ought to answer every man.
