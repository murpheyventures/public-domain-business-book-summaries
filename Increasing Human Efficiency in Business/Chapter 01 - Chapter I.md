## Summary

Muscular strength and mental efficiency seem to be fixed quantities not subject to increase or improvement. _The contention here supported, however, is that human efficiency is a variable quantity which increases and decreases according to law. At one moment one team would seem to possess much superior physical strength to the other; the next moment the equilibrium would be changed apparently without cause.
