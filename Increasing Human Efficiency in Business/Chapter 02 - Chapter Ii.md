## Summary

Teaching is by example rather than by precept, just as it was in the old apprentice system. _The newer method uses even more than the older a perfect example of the process and the product for the learner's imitation and makes them the basis of the instruction_. For the sake of clearness, in studying acts of imitation we separate them into two classes--_*voluntary_ imitation (also called conscious imitation) and _*instinctive_ imitation (also known as _*suggestive_ imitation).
